Privacy Statement

About this Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the personal data we collect about you through our website, by post, phone, email, or other forms of communication.

It explains how we process personal data and your rights as a data subject. We may update this policy to comply with legal, technical, or business changes.

What Personal Data We Collect and Why

This section informs you of what information we collect about you and why. Personal data means any information about an individual.

  • Identity Data includes first name, surname, username, marital status, title, and date of birth.
  • Contact Data includes billing address, delivery address, email address, and telephone numbers.
  • Transaction Data includes details about payments and purchases.
  • Technical Data includes IP address, login data, browser type, and operating system.
  • Usage Data includes information about how you use our website and services.
  • Marketing and Communications Data includes your preferences for receiving communications.

How We Collect Data

Direct Interaction: We collect personal data when you fill in forms, correspond with us, or purchase products.

Automated Technologies: As you interact with our website, we collect technical data using cookies, server logs, and similar technologies.

Who Will Access Your Data

Your personal data will only be seen or used by our employees and trusted third parties, such as:

  • Business partners, suppliers, and subcontractors involved in the delivery of products or services.
  • Analytics and search engine providers to improve website functionality.

How Do We Protect Your Data?

We take your privacy seriously and are committed to maintaining the privacy, security, and accuracy of your data.

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction of your data.
