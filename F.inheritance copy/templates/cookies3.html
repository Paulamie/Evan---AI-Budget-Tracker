Cookies Policy

This policy explains how our website uses cookies.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are text files containing small amounts of information that are downloaded to your personal computer,

mobile, or other device when you visit a website.

Cookies are then sent back to the originating website on each subsequent visit, or to another website that recognizes that cookie.

They are useful because they allow a website to recognize a user's device.

Cookies themselves do not identify you personally, only the computer or device you are using.

They perform various tasks, such as making it easier for you to log in and use our site during future visits,

enabling efficient navigation between pages, remembering your preferences, and generally improving your experience.

They also allow us to monitor traffic on our site and help ensure that the ads you see online are more relevant to your interests.

Cookies only record which areas of our site have been visited by your device and for how long. Allowing us to create a cookie does not give us access to the rest of your computer or allow us to view any personally identifiable data about you.

Types of Cookies

We've arranged the cookies we use into three groups:

  • a) Required cookies:
    These cookies are necessary for core features of this site to operate properly. They are always set to "Active" because they are needed for the site's operation. They include cookies that enable you to log into secure areas of our website or make a purchase.
  • b) Functionality cookies:
    These cookies allow us to analyze site usage to evaluate and improve its performance. They are also used to enhance user experience on the site, such as by measuring interactions with particular content or remembering settings.
  • c) Advertising cookies:
    These cookies are used by advertising companies to inform and serve personalized ads relevant to your interests. They may also facilitate sharing information with social networks or record your interactions with particular ads.

If You Choose Not to Use Cookies

If you choose not to accept any Functionality or Advertising cookies,
or if your browser is not compatible with our cookie preference tool, we will only use Required cookies.

Cookies That Have Been Set in the Past

If you have disabled one or more Functionality or Advertising cookies,
we may still use information collected from cookies prior to your disabled preference being set. However, we will stop using any disabled cookies to collect further information.
